Rayleigh Fading Simulator

Click the button below to simulate Rayleigh Fading.



The experiment generates a plot of the fading curve on the canvas. The fading curve represents the amplitude of the fading channel, and if the curve shows random fluctuations over time, then it is an indication that Rayleigh fading is taking place.


To test Rayleigh Fading, perform the following steps:

  • Click the "Simulate Rayleigh Fading" button to generate a random fading curve.

  • Observe the fading curve and look for random fluctuations in the amplitude of the fading channel over time.

  • Repeat the steps several times to verify that the random fluctuations are consistent and follow a Rayleigh distribution.
